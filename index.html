01
Live Client Campaign
Meta Ads Campaign
Custom T-Shirt Brand
The Problem

A custom/printed t-shirt brand needed to generate consistent, qualified demand on a limited ad budget, with no prior structured Meta Ads presence and inconsistent creative testing.

Strategy
  • Funnel Mapping: built a multi-stage buyer journey from cold discovery to add-to-cart, structuring distinct campaigns by funnel stage instead of one flat campaign.
  • Audience Segmentation: interest-based targeting, Lookalikes (LALs), and custom retargeting pools for visitors and past engagers.
  • Creative Matrix: a dynamic testing matrix rotating product-led carousels, lifestyle imagery, and short-form UGC video to find top performers before scaling spend.
Awareness➔ Traffic➔ Conversion
Execution
  • Campaign Architecture: configured campaigns in Meta Ads Manager with granular ad sets, placements, and custom budget pacing.
  • Creative Validation: Advantage+ creative frameworks and automated placement previews for optimal rendering across Feeds, Stories, Reels & Marketplace.
  • Performance Optimization: monitored CTR, CPC, and frequency daily, swapping underperforming assets to eliminate ad fatigue.
Result

Improved ad relevance and creative variety reduced wasted impressions and kept cost-per-click efficient across an expanding catalog of designs, while structured retargeting kept warm audiences engaged through to purchase.

02
Case Study
Healthcare Lead Generation Strategy
Dental & RCM Services
The Problem

Healthcare service providers (dental and revenue-cycle-management businesses) often struggle to generate a predictable flow of qualified leads while staying compliant with strict ad policies around medical claims.

Strategy
  • Built a Dental RCM campaign concept positioning the service around a clear pain point: lost revenue from claim denials and slow collections.
  • Designed a messaging hierarchy, lead headline, proof points (clean claim rate, faster insurance payments), and a single clear CTA.
  • Planned a multi-placement creative set (Feed, Stories, Reels, in-stream video) so the same offer could be tested across formats without diluting the message.
Execution
  • Created ad mockups across Facebook Feed, Instagram Stories/Reels, and Marketplace placements using Meta's Advantage+ preview tools.
  • Used a Leads Centre-style pipeline (Intake → Qualified → Converted) to plan how form-based leads would be tracked and handed off.
  • Defined KPIs upfront: cost per qualified lead, intake-to-qualified conversion rate, and lead response time.
Strategic Outcome & Target KPIs

A repeatable, compliant healthcare lead-generation framework balancing medical advertising restrictions with high-converting creative structures.

Case Study
Ayurvedic Skincare Marketing Strategy
D2C Natural Skincare
The Problem

Ayurvedic and natural-ingredient skincare brands compete in a crowded D2C space where trust, authenticity, and ingredient storytelling matter more than discount-driven offers.

Strategy
  • Positioned the brand around ingredient transparency and traditional formulation rather than competing purely on price.
  • Planned a content pillar structure: education, social proof (before/after, routines), and offer-led conversion content across the funnel.
  • Outlined an influencer-and-organic-to-paid bridge, using top-performing organic content as the base for paid amplification.
Execution Plan
  • Drafted Instagram carousel and Reel concepts around routine-building (“AM/PM ritual”) and ingredient spotlights (neem, turmeric, saffron).
  • Mapped a retargeting sequence for visitors who viewed products but didn't purchase, using testimonial-style creative to rebuild trust.
  • Defined SEO-friendly blog topics to support organic discovery for high-intent, ingredient-specific search queries.
Strategic Outcome & Target KPIs

A full-funnel organic and paid framework designed to prioritize brand trust and ingredient education over margin-cutting discounts.

Case Study
Fashion Brand Full-Funnel Strategy
Apparel & E-commerce
The Problem

Fast-fashion and apparel brands struggle to manage rapid trend cycles, frequent collection drops, and highly visual audiences across multiple channels without losing funnel discipline or over-discounting inventory.

Strategy
  • Full-Funnel Mapping: a multi-channel map bridging Awareness, Consideration, Conversion, and Retention with channel-specific tactics at each stage.
  • Calendar Alignment: seasonal campaign calendars tied to product collection drops, keeping creative direction consistent with brand identity.
  • Content Sourcing: UGC frameworks and styling-content prompts for a steady, cost-effective stream of authentic, shareable assets.
Awareness➔ Consideration➔ Conversion➔ Retention
Execution Plan
  • Creative Concepts: high-converting ad and carousel concepts for collection launches, capsule lines, and flash holiday drops.
  • Retargeting Architecture: a granular, CRM-style structure separating cart-abandoners, past purchasers, and high-intent browsers into custom sequences.
  • Asset Optimization: clear creative parameters to analyze performance across formats and visual themes.
Strategic Outcome & Target KPIs

A scalable, funnel-mapped framework treating e-commerce fashion marketing as a continuous system rather than disconnected, one-off campaigns.

05
Live Project
SEO & Organic Growth Strategy
Fashion Blog — GlamGuru
The Problem

A fashion content site needed a structured organic growth engine, most visibility relied on inconsistent posting with no real keyword targeting, on-page structure, or conversion-aware page architecture, leaving a lot of free, high-intent search traffic on the table.

Strategy
  • Keyword Research: mapped high-intent, low-competition fashion and styling queries to identify realistic ranking opportunities for a growing site.
  • On-Page Framework: built a repeatable on-page SEO checklist, title tags, meta descriptions, header hierarchy, internal linking, and image alt-text, applied consistently across every post.
  • Content Calendar: planned blog topics around seasonal trend cycles and styling guides, balancing evergreen search demand with timely content.
Execution
  • Hands-on website management on WordPress, page structuring, navigation cleanup, and landing page builds designed for conversion-focused funnels.
  • Used Google Search Console to monitor indexing, query performance, and click-through rates, feeding insights back into content updates.
  • Live deployment: glamguru.infinityfreeapp.com an active fashion blog used as the testbed for the SEO framework.
Strategic Outcome & Target KPIs

A live, working SEO foundation built from the ground up, proving the on-page and content framework on a real domain rather than a theoretical plan.
